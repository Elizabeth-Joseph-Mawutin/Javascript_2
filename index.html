Free web Africa designers award

The Free Web Africa designers award is for designers in Africa (web designers, graphic designers, UI/UX designers and video ad designers) that wants to highlight themselves to attract attention of the media, companies and customers (foreign and local) with their digital skills.

We believe that Africa as a whole is a reservoir of digitally skilled individuals, but if this skill is not appreciated it may not develop to its full potential. We believe that Africa posses the capacity to become global leaders in the technological and digital industry if her skills are harnessed to its full potential. That is the essence of the Free Web Africa designers award. The award which is given annually will encourage designers to put more effort in developing themselves and their skill, not just to emerge as a winner in the award but to deliver high quality work to their clients as well as attract international recommendations.

Schedule of events

The Free Web Africa designers awards will be held online. Participants from various categories of designs will be competing against each other. Accepted categories are web designers, graphic designers, UI/UX designers and video ad designers. Acceptable number of participants is 1000. Please be advised that the Free Web Africa designers Awards is restricted to African countries only.

Below are the schedule of events

  • Registration starts on the 15th of August and ends on the 10th of October 2022
  • Screening of designs by judges from 11th to 17th of October
  • Shortlisting of best designers for voting on the the 19th of October.
  • Voting takes place from the 19th of October to the 9th of November.
  • Shortlisting of winners on the 10th of November.
  • Issuance of Awards on the 12th of November

Judging criteria

Creativity

Creativity is the uniqueness of any breathtaking design. We want to see what makes your design sparkle! We will be judging you on how unique your design is, the layout, the branding.. All things creative.

User experience

If you are a web designer user experience should be the forefront of everything you're doing when it comes to designing. You will be judged on ease of use, function and design and mobile/desktop optimization.


Tasks for each category

Web design

You are to either create a new website or you can submit a website you’ve created before provided that its not more than 12 months old.

UI/UX design

Every country has a Covid-19 emergency response service. Your task is to design the UI of a mobile application that could be used for the service. The UI should be a maximum of 3.

Video ad design

our task is to create a 1 minute video ad to advertise any product of your choice.

Graphic design

Your task is to design a logo for a transport company. You can choose any company of your choice( Airlines are allowed).
